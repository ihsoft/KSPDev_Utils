EventCheckerCheckLeftSymmetricalModifiers Method

Verifies if the left-side "symmetrical" keyboard modifiers, defined in the event, are currently in effect (pressed and hold). The exact match is required.

Remarks
The "symmetrical modifiers" are modifier keys that are represented on the both sides of a standard 101-key keyboard and, thus, have "left" and "right" keys. These are: ALT, CTRL, SHIFT, and COMMAND.
